General Information About Dogs

Overview

Dogs are domesticated mammals, scientifically known as Canis lupus familiaris. They have been bred for thousands of years for various purposes, including hunting, herding, and companionship. Dogs are known for their loyalty and their ability to bond with humans, making them one of the most popular pets worldwide.
